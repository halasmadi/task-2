Pasta Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (about 225 grams) fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Optional garnish: Fresh parsley, chopped

    • Instructions:

  • Cook the Fettuccine: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (usually around 8-10 minutes). Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  • Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once the butter has melted, add the heavy cream. Stir constantly and allow the mixture to heat up, but don't let it come to a boil.
  • Add Parmesan Cheese: Reduce the heat to low and gradually stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Continue to stir until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce thickens. This should take about 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Be cautious with the salt, as the Parmesan cheese is already salty.
  • Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked fettuccine to the saucepan with the Alfredo sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce until it's well coated and heated through.
  • Serve: Transfer the Fettuccine Alfredo to serving plates. Optionally, garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.
  • Enjoy: Serve the Fettuccine Alfredo hot and enjoy your creamy, cheesy pasta dish!

    • Shrimp Spaghetti

    Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 1 lb (450g) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

    • Instructions:

  • Boil spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  • In the same skillet, pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens, about 2 minutes.
  • Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and stir to combine with the sauce.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss everything together until well coated with the sauce.
  • Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

    • Lasagna

    Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups (475ml) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (475ml) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    • Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  • Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft.
  • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  • Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  • Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  • Spread a third of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  • Repeat layers: sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce on top.
  • Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese on top.
  • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the lasagna is bubbly and golden brown.
  • Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
